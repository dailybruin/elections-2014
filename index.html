Jerry Brown

Party Democrat, Incumbent
Platform

Neel Kashkari

Party Republican, Former Investment Banker
Platform
  • Funding public universities based on performance measures such as graduation rates, etc.
  • Giving companies in California a tax break for creating jobs for next 10 years.
  • Promoting fracking and reforming the California Environmental Quality Act, as well as reviewing all regulations.
