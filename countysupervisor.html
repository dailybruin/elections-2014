Sheila Kuehl

Party Democrat
Biography 73 years old. Graduated from UCLA and Harvard Law School. A former child actress, and a former state legislator. She served eight years in the State Senate and six years in the State Assembly. A director of Public Policy Institute at Santa Monica College.
Platform
  • Having a system, such as bicycle lockers and buses, to get people to the nearest light rail station
  • Having a strong oversight of the Sheirff’s Department, by citizens and supervisors
  • Hiring more social workers for child and foster care

Bobby Shriver

Party Democrat
Biography 60 years old. Graduated from Yale and Yale Law School. A former journalist and law clerk. He worked with Bono to found PRODUCT (RED) in 2006 to help fight the AIDS epidemic in Africa. Also served as a mayor of Santa Monica in 2010.
Platform
  • Expanding bike-sharing and car-sharing programs and having a rail line directly to LAX
  • Making it easier for businesses to obtain permits, licensing and documents
  • Capturing stormwater and monitoring water usage to promote water conservation
