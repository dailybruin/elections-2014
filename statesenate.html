Ben Allen

Party Democrat, Attorney and Lecturer at UCLA School of Law
Platform
  • Prioritizing state funding for the universities and giving the legislature a stronger voice in funding higher education
  • Training workers and keeping jobs in the region through incentives while restoring funds to welfare programs
  • Moratorium on “fracking,” and conserving water through recycling, rainwater diversion, etc.

Sandra Fluke

Party Democrat, Social Justice Attorney
Platform
  • Funding public state universities at a higher level while putting a greater emphasis on science, technology, engineering and mathematics programs.
  • Investing in start-up companies and training students to have technological skills needed for jobs in those companies.
  • Having a state audit of state employees to examine whether both men and women are paid equally for same work; extending paid family leave policy.
